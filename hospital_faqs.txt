Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What are the hospital operating hours?
A: The hospital operates 24/7 for emergency services. OPD services are available from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

Q: Do I need an appointment to see a doctor?
A: Walk-ins are accepted, but appointments are recommended to reduce waiting time.

Q: Do you accept international health insurance?
A: Yes, we accept major international and local insurance providers.

Q: Are diagnostic tests available on-site?
A: Yes, laboratory tests, MRI, CT scans, and ECG services are available.

Q: Do you provide emergency care?
A: Yes, 24/7 emergency and trauma care services are available.
